About

The Shops at Sawmill Glade will serve as a vital hub of resources for the community located at the intersection of Old FM 471 and Sawmill Glade, a stone's throw away from Culebra Rd. This expansive development endeavors to cater to diverse industries, ranging from general retail and hospitality to education and medical professionals.

With Misty Baker and Tim Little at the helm of this project, the community couldn't have hoped for better developers. Their unwavering commitment to bringing a sustainable and aesthetically pleasing building to the area is truly commendable. Backed by their wealth of experience and expertise, the area is set to reap the benefits of this groundbreaking development.

Meet the Developers

Misty Baker

Misty is a native San Antonian and avid sports fan. Mom to 9 beautiful children, loves the outdoors, and is obsessed with the world of commercial construction and development.

Misty's been around construction for the last 13 years and has specialized in commercial development for the past 6 years. Her history includes Medical Professional Offices in San Antonio and New Braunfels, an Executive Private Airplane Hangar in Boerne, general retail projects in Alamo Ranch and large scale underground utility installation projects like a phase of the RIM.

Misty's claim to fame among those that have worked with her is that she's one of the best at value-engineering projects. She also brings the attention to detail and call to order over her projects that only a mother of 9 can bring to the management of a construction project.

Learn more about Misty and her business at mistycontracting.com

Tim Little

Tim is a native of Devine, Texas and you could say that he's been around construction since he was born. Tim grew up on a farm and went to work with his uncle in the summers as a framer since he was 11 so that he could afford his own school clothes for the following school year.

Tim's been involved in commercial and residential construction for over 50 years now and has become more accutely focused on commercial development for the last 6 years. You might have an easier time finding projects in San Antonio that Tim hasn't been involved in to some degree than finding the ones he has been involved in. There's likely not a better site guy in Texas.

Tim's an avid outdoorsman and loves fishing off the coast. He's not one for many words but he's always in for a good laugh and a fun time, especially if a bonfire or barbecue is going down.
